NaukriMitra Platform

A scalable recruitment platform designed to enable real-time job tracking, location-based matching, and seamless communication between recruiters and candidates.

Problem

Recruitment processes lacked real-time tracking, centralized dashboards, and efficient location-based job matching, leading to delays and poor user experience.

Solution

Built a modern job platform powered by API-driven architecture with real-time communication and geolocation-based job discovery features.

Architecture

  • Admin & HR dashboards
  • API-based job management system
  • Google Maps integration for location tracking
  • Real-time communication APIs
